
# Vietnam Tax & Legal Reference

Use this reference when building financial models or performing valuations for Vietnamese companies.

## Corporate Income Tax (CIT)

| Rate | Applicability |
|------|-------------|
| **20%** | Standard rate for all enterprises (since 2016) |
| **10% for 15 years** | High-tech zones, prioritized industrial parks |
| **17%** | Standard industrial parks, encouraged sectors |
| **32-50%** | Oil & gas exploration, mineral extraction |
| **Exemption 2-4 years** | New projects in priority sectors |

**Always check** the company-specific effective tax rate in BCTC/TMBS — many companies have blended rates due to subsidiaries in different zones.

## Capital Gains Tax

| Type | Rate | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Stock trading** | **0.1% on selling price** | Flat rate, regardless of gain/loss |
| **Real estate** | 2% on transfer price | OR 20% on gain if documented |
| **Dividend income (individual)** | 5% flat | Withholding at source |
| **Dividend income (corporate)** | 0% | Inter-corporate dividends exempt |

> **Key insight:** VN capital gains tax on stocks is 0.1% on SELLING PRICE — not on profit. This fundamentally changes tax-loss harvesting strategies compared to US.

## Foreign Ownership Limits (FOL)

| Sector | FOL |
|--------|-----|
| **Default (general)** | 49% |
| **Banking** | 30% (single investor 15%) |
| **Securities companies** | 49% (under review for increase) |
| **Real estate (conditional)** | 49% |
| **Defense, media, telecom** | 0% |
| **Some sectors post-reform** | Up to 100% (EVFTA sectors) |

**Impact on valuation:**
- Companies near FOL limit trade at premium (foreign demand blocked)
- "Room mở" (FOL expansion) = positive catalyst
- Companies with low FOL have lower liquidity from foreign funds

## Transfer Pricing

- **Regulation:** Nghị định 132/2020/NĐ-CP (effective 2020)
- Aligned with OECD BEPS guidelines
- **Related-party transaction disclosure** in TMBS (Notes to FS)
- **Red flag:** High intercompany transactions → check TMBS Section "Giao dịch bên liên quan"

## M&A Regulatory Framework

| Requirement | Authority | Threshold |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| Competition approval | Bộ Công Thương | Combined market share >50% |
| SSC approval | Ủy ban Chứng khoán (UBCKNN) | All listed company acquisitions |
| SBV approval | Ngân hàng Nhà nước | Banking sector M&A |
| Foreign investment registration | Sở KH&ĐT | New FDI or increase >51% |

## Land Use Rights (Quyền Sử Dụng Đất — QSDĐ)

- Vietnam does **NOT** have freehold land ownership
- All land belongs to the State; companies hold **Land Use Rights** (QSDĐ)
- QSDĐ has a fixed term (typically 50-70 years for industrial, 50 years for residential)
- **Impact:** QSDĐ should be amortized in models over remaining term
- Real estate valuation: NAV approach must consider QSDĐ remaining term
- **Check:** TMBS for QSDĐ expiration dates and book values

## Social Insurance (BHXH) — Labor Costs

| Contribution | Employer | Employee |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| Social insurance (BHXH) | 17.5% | 8% |
| Health insurance (BHYT) | 3% | 1.5% |
| Unemployment (BHTN) | 1% | 1% |
| **Total** | **21.5%** | **10.5%** |

Base: Capped at 20× mức lương cơ sở (do Chính phủ quy định, cập nhật hàng năm theo Nghị định). Luôn kiểm tra mức hiện hành tại thời điểm phân tích.
